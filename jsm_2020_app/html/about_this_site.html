With the help of many, Wendy compiled and cleaned a dataset of 106 addresses delivered by ASA presidents dating back to 1908. This dataset includes full text of each address, images of the president who delivered it, aspects of their professional lives, and details about the conference at which their speech was delivered. This site presents this unique dataset in an accessible way.

On the following pages, you can:

  • Speech Text Analysis - Select any ASA president back to 1908 and see an analysis of the address they delivered that year.
  • Compare All Speeches - Compare speeches on a range of metrics from readability to sentiment to themes.
  • Word/Topics Through Time - See how the use of certain words or the presence of certain themes has changed over the last 111 years.
  • Conference Information - As an added bonus, select a year back to 1908 and see details about conference dates, themes, locations, and more.
  • About the Data - Read about where the data used in this app came from and how they were cleaned.
